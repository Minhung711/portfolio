3. Turnover rate in Ha Noi Capital

Figure 3.1.1

Observing the bar graph titled "Figure 3.1.1" provides a clear depiction of the turnover rates within Ha Noi Capital across distinct business sectors. The highest turnover rate is observed in the "WSS-Chuyển giao Tài sản tỉnh" sector, standing at 48.25%. This is marginally surpassed by the "GCC-Chuyển giao Cơ sở hạ tầng tỉnh" sector at 47.27%, indicating a similarly high level of asset liquidity or transfer activities. The "WTT-Chuyển giao Công ty đô thị" comes in third with a significant drop, posting a turnover rate of 33.33%. Finally, the "ITS￾Chuyển giao Phần mềm" sector has the lowest turnover rate among the presented categories at 16.42%. This graphical representation suggests a variance in turnover dynamics that could be reflective of differing market conditions, capital intensities, or business activity frequencies across these sectors. It would be essential for a Data Analyst to further investigate the underlying causes driving these rates, such as market demand, investment levels, regulatory impacts, or other economic factors specific to the Ha Noi Capital region.
